# Dental Vertical Offer

## Dental Vertical Offer (In Progress — NOT Finalized as of March 2026)

### Target
- Cosmetic dental practices — porcelain veneers, full-mouth reconstruction, smile design, high-ticket elective procedures
- NOT general dentistry — the offer is built around cosmetic/elective case generation
- Economics: $15K average case value, ~$8K profit per veneer case
- Offer status: ClickUp task "Dental Practice offer dialed in" overdue since March 20, 2026 — offer NOT finalized

### Dr. Laleh Case Study (Anchor)
- Grew consultations from 50-60/month to 105+
- Revenue from $1M to $2M (adding $200K+ monthly profit)
- Previous agency spent $100-110K/month WITHOUT growing past 60 consultations
- CPA dropped from $48.79 to $9.58 (80% reduction)
- Conversions grew from 26 to 413 per year
- Dr. Laleh is $6,000/month (not $2,000-$3,000 — signed before price increases)

### Three Offer Models Being Compared
(A) Pay-per-consultation $250-$500 — primary model being considered, with "no charge until profitable" framing
(B) Hybrid base $1,500-$2,000 + $100-$150 kicker per consultation above baseline — EXPERT RECOMMENDED
(C) Modified % of spend

### Andrew Street Warning
Andrew Street (No Luck Leads / Ad Sync) warned Peterson against pure performance pricing — payment disputes, bad client relationships. Recommended hybrid: $150/booked appointment + client pays ad spend, OR $75/appointment + $1K/month retainer.

### CAUTION on "No Charge Until Profitable"
This framing attracts risk-averse clients who can't afford to invest. The practices Creekside should target ($1M+ revenue, established patient base) don't need risk removal — they need confidence. Lead with the Dr. Laleh case study, not the safety net.
