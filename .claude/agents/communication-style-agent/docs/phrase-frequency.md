## Client Pushback Phrases
| Phrase | Function |
|--------|----------|
| "I'm interested to hear from your team why..." | Challenges client assumption politely |
| "Profitability doesn't always equal scalability" | Educational reframing |
| "That would be more recency bias than an actual trend" | Data-grounded correction |
| "Not here to make excuses... just want to ask that you give us time" | Honest + asks for patience |
| "My preference is always not to mess with something that is working" | Frames recommendation as conservative |
| "If you aren't comfortable with us testing... we will stop now, won't charge you" | Gives client a real out |
