# Discovery Framework and Sales Process

## 11-Step Discovery Framework

Use this to understand what context to extract from Fathom call transcripts before generating a proposal.

1. **Rapid response** -- Peterson's standard: call within the first hour of inquiry
2. **Pre-call warm-up** -- send intro video and 4 qualifying questions before the call
3. **Opening** -- "Tell me about your business and what you're looking for"
4. **5-minute qualifier** -- ad spend, urgency, decision makers, timeline
5. **Discovery questions** -- budget, history, failures, goals, capacity
6. **Deep-dive** -- expand pain points, get consequences, get vision of success
7. **Live account audit** -- spend 10 minutes in their account, find issues, give free feedback
8. **Present strategy** -- Canva deck with relevant case studies (proactively in first 15 minutes)
9. **Pricing** -- wait for them to ask before presenting; present tiered structure
10. **Close** -- trial close, present pricing, ask "what would it take to move forward this week?" then SILENCE
11. **Follow-up cadence** -- Day 0: recap email; Day 3: relevant case study; Day 7: direct ask; Day 14: final nudge; monthly nurture after that

## What to Extract from Fathom Transcripts

When pulling a Fathom transcript for proposal personalization, look for these specific items:

| Item | Where to find it | How to use it |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly ad budget | Usually stated in step 4 or 5 of the call | Drives plan selection math |
| Business goals | Step 5/6 -- "what does success look like?" | Customize Month 3 of 90-day plan |
| Current state | Step 3/4 -- "tell me about your current ads" | Customize Month 1 of 90-day plan |
| Previous agency failures | Step 5 -- "what didn't work before?" | Address in the Current State section |
| Objections raised | Step 9/10 -- noted as hesitations or questions | Do NOT put in proposal; save for follow-up call notes |
| Specific commitments Peterson made | Any point in the call -- "we'll set up X", "I'll send you Y" | Echo verbatim in Onboarding Services + Next Steps |
| Decision makers | Step 4 -- "is it just you deciding?" | Address the proposal to the right person |
| Urgency / timeline | Step 4 -- "when do you need this live?" | Customize timeline in Next Steps |
| Industry | Step 3 -- "tell me about your business" | Match to case study below |

## Follow-Up Cadence (reference only -- not for the proposal body)

Day 0 (same day as call): Recap email + proposal PDF
Day 3: Send relevant case study if not already shared
Day 7: Direct ask -- "Are you ready to move forward?"
Day 14: Final nudge -- "Wanted to check in before we close this slot out"
Monthly after that: Light nurture until they become a yes or a definitive no

This cadence is for Peterson's reference. It does not go into the proposal.
