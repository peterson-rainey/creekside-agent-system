# Hero Video Outline: 60-90 Seconds (Audit Funnel)
## Dr. Laleh Case Study + Account Walkthrough

**Purpose:** This video serves triple duty -- hero on the audit landing page, case study ad creative, and account walkthrough proof.

**Format:** Sound bites over screen content (Jybr style). NOT a continuous talking head. Peterson (or Cade) records the talking head intro and CTA bookends, plus all the sound bites as separate clips. Editor splices the sound bites over screen recordings, screenshots, and visuals.

**Pace:** ~195 wpm
**Target length:** 60-90 seconds

---

## STRUCTURE

### [TALKING HEAD -- ~10 sec]
> "We took Dr. Laleh's cosmetic dental practice from one million to two million in revenue a month within six months. Same ad budget. Let me show you exactly what that looks like."

---

### [SOUND BITES OVER VISUALS -- ~50-70 sec]

Record each of these as a separate clip. The editor will lay them over the corresponding visuals.

**Over Dr. Laleh's Instagram / social proof (follower count, her content):**
> "Dr. Laleh runs a cosmetic and implant dental practice in California. Great organic following. Over 190,000 followers. She was already good at marketing."

**Over Slack / message screenshots (real conversations showing results):**
> "She was spending a hundred and ten thousand a month on ads with a previous agency and stuck. Fifty to sixty consultations a month. They couldn't move the needle."

**Over ad account dashboards (Meta spend, Google spend):**
> "We rebuilt her entire ad strategy. Around a hundred thousand a month on Meta, thirteen to fifteen thousand on Google. High volume at the top, convert on search."

**Over results (consultation numbers, revenue growth):**
> "We don't optimize for leads. We optimize for revenue. Patients in chairs. That took her from fifty consultations a month to over a hundred and five."

**Over the "record high 100 consults" message:**
> "Over two hundred thousand in additional monthly profit. Same budget. She's since started a second business with all the time we freed up."

**Over calculator (sliders moving, ROAS numbers changing):**
> "And it's not just about her budget. This works at any spend level. The math is the same whether you're spending five thousand a month or a hundred thousand."

---

### [TALKING HEAD -- ~10 sec]
> "Want to know what's going wrong in your ads? Book a free audit with our team. We'll pull up your account live and tell you exactly what to fix. 30 minutes. Link's below."

## EDITING NOTES

- Sound bites are modular. Editor can drop any section to hit 60 seconds or keep all for 90.
- Key numbers should get text overlay emphasis when spoken (5.7X, 105 consultations, $200K+, $1M to $2M).
- Music: subtle, modern. Same feel as Jybr videos.
- End card: Creekside branding + "Book Your Free Ad Account Audit" + URL
- Primary aspect ratio: 16:9 (landing page embed). Also need 9:16 and 1:1 cuts for ads.
