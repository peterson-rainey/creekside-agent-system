# Facebook Ad Copy: Dental Practice Offer (Audit Funnel) -- V2
## For Aamir / Cade

**V2 (2026-07-10):** Launch set cut from 13 ads to 5, each paired 1:1 with a v2 static creative (see static-ad-creative-briefs-audit.md). Changes driven by 90-day account data: guarantee/hard-offer structure leads (mirrors the account's cheapest converter), proof copy reframed from "what's wrong with your ads" to "how we'd do the same for your practice," emotional angle kept and funded (highest CTR signal, never tested past $63). Ads 12-13 kept unchanged for Phase 2.

**Funnel Role:** Ads catch attention. The prospect's mindset after seeing the ad should be "these people look like they could help me." The landing page convinces. The audit delivers value and converts. Ads do NOT need to tell the full story -- they hook and get the click.

**Specs:** Primary text ~125 chars visible before "See More." Headline 27-40 chars. No em dashes.

## TESTING PLAN

**Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2):**
- Launch Ads 1, 2, 4, 5 with their paired v2 statics (cold traffic). Ad 1 (guarantee) and Ad 2 (proof) get priority budget.
- Ad 4 (emotional) gets a real budget this time: $500 minimum for a conversion read.
- Test each with 1,000+ impressions before judging.

**Phase 2 (Weeks 3-4):**
- Add Ad 3 (needs the Laleh screenshot asset)
- Add Ads 12, 13 (copy only, reuse v2 statics)
- Kill bottom 2 performers from Phase 1, scale winners

**Phase 3 (Month 2):**
- Retargeting: show Ads 2 and 3 to LP visitors who didn't book
- Lookalike audience from audit bookers
- Layer in Cade's videos once recorded (guarantee-hook Laleh proof script first, detail targeting not broad)

**Primary Audience (all ads):**
- Job titles: Dentist, Practice Owner, DDS, DMD, Dental Practice Manager, Marketing Director

- Behaviors: business page admins (likely practice owners)
- Age: 30-65
- Location: US-wide, California emphasis
