**Funnel Role:** Ads catch attention. The prospect's mindset after seeing the ad should be "these people look like they could help me." The landing page convinces. The audit delivers value and converts. Ads do NOT need to tell the full story -- they hook and get the click.

**Specs:** Primary text ~125 chars visible before "See More." Headline 27-40 chars. Description max 30 chars (AI-generated, rarely shown on mobile). No em dashes.

**All ads:** CTA Button = Learn More, Destination = audit landing page (creeksidemarketingpros.com/dental-ads-audit/).

## AUDIENCE TARGETING

**Primary Audience (all ads):**
- Job titles: Dentist, Practice Owner, DDS, DMD, Dental Practice Manager, Marketing Director
- Interests: dental practice management, cosmetic dentistry, ADA, dental conferences, dental marketing
- Behaviors: business page admins (likely practice owners)
- Age: 30-65
- Location: US-wide, California emphasis
- Exclude: dental students, dental assistants, dental hygienists, dental lab technicians
