## TESTING PLAN

**Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2):**
- Launch Ads 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 9, 10, 11 in primary ad set (cold traffic)
- Launch Ad 8 separately (Dr. Laleh follower targeting)
- Test each with 1,000+ impressions before judging

**Phase 2 (Weeks 3-4):**
- Add Ads 6, 7 (these need creative assets -- screenshots, photos)
- Kill bottom 3-4 performers from Phase 1
- Scale winners

**Phase 3 (Month 2):**
- Retargeting campaign: show Ad 4 and Ad 7 to audit landing page visitors who didn't book
- Lookalike audience from audit bookers
- Test video ads once Cade records them

## AUDIENCE TARGETING (Unchanged)

**Primary Audience (all ads except Ad 8):**
- Job titles: Dentist, Practice Owner, DDS, DMD, Dental Practice Manager, Marketing Director
- Interests: dental practice management, cosmetic dentistry, ADA, dental conferences, dental marketing
- Behaviors: business page admins (likely practice owners)
- Age: 30-65
- Location: US-wide, California emphasis
- Exclude: dental students, dental assistants, dental hygienists, dental lab technicians

**Dr. Laleh Follower Audience (Ad 8 only):**
- Custom audience: followers of Dr. Laleh's Facebook/Instagram pages
- Lookalike: 1% lookalike of Dr. Laleh's follower base
- Layer with: job title (dentist, DDS, DMD) to filter out patients
