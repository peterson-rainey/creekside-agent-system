At $5,500 you don't have enough to split across two platforms and get meaningful data on either. Google needs at least $3,000/month to optimize properly, and so does Facebook. Splitting $5,500 two ways puts both below that floor and you end up learning slowly on both instead of learning fast on one.

Google is the right starting point for a moving company because people search when they need to move. That intent is already there. Facebook would be more about generating awareness with people who aren't actively looking yet, which works but takes more budget and longer timelines to see ROI.
